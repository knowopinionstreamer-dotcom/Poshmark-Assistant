# How to Use the Poshmark Assistant

This guide will walk you through using the Poshmark Assistant to create a listing from start to finish.

### Step 1: Upload Your Product Images

The process begins by uploading one or more photos of your item.

1.  Drag and drop your image files onto the upload area, or click to select them from your computer.
2.  The application will automatically begin analyzing the images.

*A screenshot showing the initial state of the image uploader.*
`![Image Uploader](docs/images/01-uploader.png)`

### Step 2: Review Item Details

Once the analysis is complete, the "Item Details" form will be populated with the information extracted from your photos (e.g., brand, color, style).

1.  Review the automatically filled fields for accuracy.
2.  Make any necessary corrections or additions.

*A screenshot of the Item Details form filled out by the AI.*
`![Item Details](docs/images/02-details.png)`

### Step 3: Research Pricing

Use the AI-powered search tools to find the market value for your item.

1.  Click the "AI Text Search" or "AI Visual Search" buttons.
2.  The assistant will generate a list of highly specific search queries.
3.  Click these links to see results on Google, Poshmark, eBay, etc.
4.  Based on your research, enter your desired price in the "Set Your Price" field.

*A screenshot showing the generated search queries and the price field.*
`![Pricing Research](docs/images/03-pricing.png)`

### Step 4: Generate the Listing Draft

With all the information gathered, you can now generate the final listing.

1.  Click the "Generate Listing Draft" button.
2.  The AI will write a compelling title and description for your Poshmark listing.
3.  You can copy this text directly into Poshmark!

*A screenshot of the final, generated listing draft with title and description.*
`![Listing Draft](docs/images/04-draft.png)`
